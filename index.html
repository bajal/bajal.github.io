    About Me

    Hello! my name is Bajal. I am from Calicut in a small state called Kerala, in India. I fell in love with coding during my teenage years when my father brought home an old dilapidated computer that his office was trying to recycle. It was running Windows 95, and it came with Michael Jackson's complete Album Thriller. The game Dangerous Dave enticed me to make some games on my own. I started fiddling around in BASIC, and like many - proceeded to make a hangman clone, and some sick tunes with QBASIC's BEEP command, much to the admonishment of my mother.

    Time passed, I graduated from a university that happens to be in the my home district itself. (National Institute of Technology, Calicut). The college computer lab was my favorite spot to hang around. The computers there ran Solaris, and this is where I learned to use the Unix Shell. After several wget s of large files that ran overnight, and exchanging notes with classmates using netcat, I finally started doing some HTML, JavaScript and Java coding in the first year.

    What I do now

    I have been a professional programmer for about 11 years now, working mainly on the backend, but some amount of frontend work as well. Coding has become the bread-winner for me and my family.
